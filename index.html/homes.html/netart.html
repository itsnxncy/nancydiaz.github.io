Your story begins in a small town. Multiple murders have happened at the carnival. To begin solving this murder mystery, first choose your detective. The first detective which is option one is Tom. He is a very skill detective, has a sharp mind for forensic details. The second option is detective Sandy. She is a forensic psychologist that can read into a killer's mind, she is definitely more empathetic. Whichever option you choose determines how the story goes and how this murder mystery is solved, if it can be solved. Choose your character wisely.
